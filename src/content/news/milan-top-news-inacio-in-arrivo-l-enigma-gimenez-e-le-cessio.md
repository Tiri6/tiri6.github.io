Market dynamics at Milan involve several prospects from the Rossoneri academy. Francesco Camarda and Christian Comotto appear set to leave the club, signaling a potential reorganization of Milan's youth structure.

Meanwhile, the Rossoneri maintain attention on external profiles like Samuele Inacio, seeking to strengthen their youth system. Contract renewal negotiations with other squad elements have also emerged. These moves reflect Milan's generational renewal strategy, with an eye on future needs and competitive requirements in coming years.

With 2030 on the horizon, targeted choices matter: departures should be evaluated as opportunities to create space for prospects with greater potential, while strategic signings must ensure solid development of the technical foundation.

Serie B continues to see significant movement in the young talent market. Giacomo Faticanti has found his new home at Avellino, completing an anticipated transfer. Meanwhile, Giuseppe Ambrosino is drawing closer to a Modena return, with the yellow-and-blues showing concrete interest.

The transfer window brings developments elsewhere too: Juve Stabia are targeting Dominic Vavassori, a further sign of growing attention on this young prospect. Italian second division continues its process of redistributing emerging talents, with important growth prospects in sight ahead of Italy's medium-term goals.

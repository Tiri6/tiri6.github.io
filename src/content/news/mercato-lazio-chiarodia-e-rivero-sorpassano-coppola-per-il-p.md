Lazio is restructuring its defensive setup and increasingly focusing on young talent to fill the void left by Romagnoli. According to the latest market reports, Fabio Chiarodia and Rivero are gaining ground over Francesco Coppola in the Biancocelesti's considerations, as the club evaluates its options among emerging Italian talent.

The two rising prospects represent alternative choices to what had been Coppola as the primary target. Lazio continues its search for defensive reinforcements capable of offering medium-to-long-term stability, consistent with the club's ambitions to build a competitive squad.

These market movements underscore the growing emphasis within Italian football on young talent—a strategic necessity as the sport looks toward the 2030 World Cup horizon.

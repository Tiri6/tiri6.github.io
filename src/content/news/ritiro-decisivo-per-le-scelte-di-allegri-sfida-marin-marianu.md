Marin e Marianucci sono al centro di questa competizione interna che, al di là dell'immediato, assume un valore strategico più ampio. The training camp represents a crucial moment for technical assessments. Allegri has chosen to focus on the defensive line, where two young Italian prospects are vying for an important position.

The natural selection that emerges on the pitch during preparation is a fundamental tool for identifying the profiles to develop on the path toward upcoming international commitments in the coming years.

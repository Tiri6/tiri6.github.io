La società campana ha completato un'operazione di mercato articolata su tre fronti, annunciando l'ingaggio di Martinelli, Moruzzi e Faticanti. I dettagli contrattuali variano da giocatore a giocatore, secondo le specifiche esigenze tecniche e gestionali del club.

The Campania-based club has completed a multifaceted market operation, announcing the acquisition of Martinelli, Moruzzi and Faticanti. The contractual terms vary from player to player, tailored to the club's specific technical and managerial needs.

For Martinelli and Faticanti in particular, the move to Avellino represents a significant opportunity in their development pathway toward higher categories. Serie B continues to serve as a valuable training ground for those aiming to establish themselves in competitive football and pursue Italy's 2030 World Cup ambitions.

Una scelta strategica che rientra nel percorso di costruzione della base calcistica torinese.

Torino continues its policy of developing young talents through loan moves in Serie B. Among the players closely monitored is Alessio Cacciamani, part of a significant group of prospects sent to gain experience in Italy's second tier. A strategic choice that fits into the club's broader project of building its competitive foundation.

These development paths represent a crucial stage for players destined to become prominent figures in the coming years. Time spent in Serie B allows young prospects to accumulate valuable minutes and strengthen the technical and mental foundations necessary to compete at higher levels.

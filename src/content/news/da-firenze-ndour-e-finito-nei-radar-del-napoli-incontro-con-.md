La dirigenza partenopea ha intrapreso contatti diretti con gli agenti del calciatore, esplorando la possibilità di un trasferimento in azzurro. Napoli is intensifying its search for promising Italian young talents, targeting Cher Ndour currently at Fiorentina. The Campania-based club has opened direct negotiations with the player's representatives, exploring a potential move to the Partenopei colors. Signals emerging from Ndour's camp are positive, with the winger showing openness to joining Napoli.

This move aligns with Napoli's strategy of strengthening the squad with young and talented profiles, with an eye on building Italy's future national team. Ndour represents one of the prospects Serie A is banking on as part of the Azzurri's project toward the 2030 World Cup.

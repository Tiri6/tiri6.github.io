Torino continues on its generational renewal path. The arrivals of Fortini and Mascardi represent another step in the club's strategy focused on young Italian talent. The choice reflects strategic continuity under Abate's leadership.

The two prospects join an ambitious project aimed at building a solid foundation for the Piedmont club's future. The investment in developing players demonstrates a commitment to long-term planning, with an eye toward the competitions awaiting the national team in the coming years.

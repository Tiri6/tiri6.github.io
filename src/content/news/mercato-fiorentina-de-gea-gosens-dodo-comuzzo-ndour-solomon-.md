Fiorentina's management is outlining its transfer strategy with a project focused on Italian young talents. Paratici has confirmed interest in several profiles, including defender Pietro Comuzzo, midfielder Cher Ndour, and forward Luca Koleosho. The Tuscan club's objective appears clear: build a competitive squad while maintaining a solid foundation of Italian prospects.

The three youngsters represent different tactical solutions for the Viola project. Their potential stay or arrival in Tuscany could represent an important piece in building a generational core capable of competing at the highest levels, with an eye also on the opportunities that will come with Italy's upcoming international commitments.
